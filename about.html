Who are we?

We are a team of young people who happen to like lots of travelling and nature. It all started when the founder and CEO of FRIO travels, Mr. Siddhant Ganesh was in his last year in college. He along with his friends planned a trip to Goa beach. But at that time, the online booking procedure was not popular and also the Holidays package companies protocols were quite tedious to understand. So Siddhant along with his classmates started this bussiness mainly for the enjoyment of the youth.
